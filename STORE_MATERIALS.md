# Lumi Retouch Store Materials

## Short description

Local-first portrait retouch editor with beauty tools, studio backgrounds, transparent PNG cutouts, presets, and batch export.

## Beta notes

Lumi Retouch beta focuses on on-device portrait editing. Use Demo to try the editor immediately, or import a photo, apply Auto, tune Beauty/Makeup/Templates, then export PNG or JPEG. No photo upload or account is required.

## Screenshot checklist

- First screen with Import and Sample.
- Auto-enhanced portrait preview.
- Beauty controls with heal brush and relight.
- Makeup controls with lip and eye tools.
- Template carousel with studio background.
- Transparent PNG cutout controls.
- Export panel with save, share, recipe, batch, benchmark, and device profile status.

## Support channel placeholder

Use the beta feedback channel configured in the Play Console closed test track. Until then, collect manual QA feedback in the project issue tracker.

## Store-facing privacy line

Photos are processed locally on your device. Lumi Retouch does not include account sync, remote analytics, or hidden image uploads in this beta build.
